Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance. For weddings and large events, 2-3 months is ideal to ensure availability.

What areas do you serve?

We serve in Lagos and all surrounding areas. Nigeria-wide service is available with travel arrangements.

Do you provide the alcohol?

We provide professional bartending services. Alcohol can be provided by the client or we can assist with procurement.

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes professional bartenders, equipment, setup, cleanup, and basic mixers. Custom quotes include all details.
