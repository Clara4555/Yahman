Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you book our services, including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Event details (date, location, number of guests)
  • Payment information for service bookings
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and manage our bartending services
  • Process payments and send booking confirmations
  • Communicate with you about your event
  • Send promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Improve our services and customer experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who assist in delivering our services
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to certain processing activities
