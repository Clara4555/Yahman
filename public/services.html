Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How far in advance should I book your services?

+

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for weddings and 2-3 weeks for other events. However, we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

Do you provide the alcohol, or do we need to supply it?

+

We offer both options! You can choose our full-service package where we provide all alcohol, mixers, and garnishes, or you can supply the alcohol and we'll provide the bartending service, mixers, and garnishes.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve the Kingston metropolitan area and surrounding parishes. For events outside our standard service area, we charge a travel fee based on distance.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or preferences?

+

Absolutely! We're experienced in creating cocktails for various dietary needs, including non-alcoholic options, gluten-free, and low-sugar alternatives. Just let us know your requirements during the booking process.
